Ibrahim Bereket Danielle Who`s that Pokémon? Pokédex

Gotta catch 'em all!

Everything to know about your favorite Pokémon and a fun little game.
Design:
We wanted to immerse the user in the Pokémon universe, so we decided to imitate the Pokédex. And, we attempted to give the user a nostalgic feeling of watching the Pokémon cartoons by creating the 'Who`s that Pokémon?' game.
Challenges:
One of our main challenges was aligning all of the elements of the Pokédex. Another challenge was implementing a lightning effect. Unfortunately, we were unable to figure it out.
